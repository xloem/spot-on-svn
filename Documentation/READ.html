Adding Participants

Repleo Exchange
  1. Obtain the public key from your friend. Paste the key in the Add Friend textedit widget of the Neighbors tab and press the Add button.
  2. Copy your Repleo for this friend from the context menu in the Participants table of the Chat tab.
  3. Send the Repleo to your friend.
Repleo Exchange
  1. Add a listener or a neighbor. Please see the Neighbors and Settings tabs.
  2. Send the IP information to your friend.
  3. Once connected, both users may share their keys via context menus in the Neighbors table.
  4. Approve of new participants via the context menu in the Participants table.
